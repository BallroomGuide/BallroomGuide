<header>American Spin (Bronze)
----------------------

 </header>Commence in Open Position with L to R Hand Hold

### Man

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Steps** | **Turn** | **Action** |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | LF back |  | Rock Step |
| 2 | Replace weight to RF |  |
| 3 | LF fwd, small step, part weight |  | Compact Chasse |
| 4 | RF closes halfway to LF |  |
| 5 | LF fwd, small step |  |
| 6 | RF fwd, small step, part weight |  | Compact Chasse |
| 7 | LF closes halfway to RF |  |
| 8 | RF fwd, small step |  |

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Timing** | **Rhythm** | **Position** | **Footwork** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1 | Q | Open | BF |
| 2 | 2 | Q | Open | BF |
| 3 | 3 | Q | Open | B |
| 4 | a | a | Open | B |
| 5 | 4 | Q | Open | BF |
| 6 | 1 | Q |  | B |
| 7 | a | a |  | B |
| 8 | 2 | Q | Open | BF |

**Method 1:** Lead the Lady to turn with pressure through his L hand (or R hand, if danced with R to R hand hold) on step 5, then release hold entirely and take the Lady's R hand at the end of her turn  
 **Method 2:** Raise the L hand over steps 3-5 and turn the Lady underarm on step 5, lowering the L hand at the end of her turn

1\) (Silver) Steps 1-2 may be replaced by a [Kick Ball Change](../technique/j_kick_ball_change.md), [Point Ball Change](../technique/j_point_ball_change.md), or [Hesitation Ball Change](../technique/j_hesitation_ball_change.md)
