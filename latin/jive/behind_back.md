<header>Change Hands Behind Back (PreBronze)
------------------------------------

 </header>Commence in Open Position with L to R Hand Hold

### Man

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Steps** | **Turn** | **Action** |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | LF back |  | Rock Step |
| 2 | Replace weight to RF |  |
| 3 | LF fwd, part weight |  | Fwd Chasse |
| 4 | RF closes halfway to LF |  |
| 5 | LF fwd | 1/8 to L over 3-5 |
| 6 | RF diag fwd, part weight |  | Turning Chasse |
| 7 | LF closes halfway to RF |  |
| 8 | RF back | 3/8 to L over 6-8 |

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Timing** | **Rhythm** | **Position** | **Footwork** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1 | Q | Open | BF |
| 2 | 2 | Q | Open | BF |
| 3 | 3 | Q |  | B |
| 4 | a | a |  | B |
| 5 | 4 | Q |  | BF |
| 6 | 1 | Q |  | B |
| 7 | a | a |  | B |
| 8 | 2 | Q | Open | BF |

**Method 1:** The Man may place the Lady's R hand in his R hand on step 2, then pass the Lady's R hand behind his back, taking it again in his L hand while behind his back on step 5  
 **Method 2:** The Man may place the Lady's R hand on the R side of his waist, releasing it and allowing it to trail along his waist over steps 3-4, taking it again in his L hand over steps 6-8

1\) (Silver) Steps 1-2 may be replaced by a [Kick Ball Change](../technique/j_kick_ball_change.md), [Point Ball Change](../technique/j_point_ball_change.md), or [Hesitation Ball Change](../technique/j_hesitation_ball_change.md)
