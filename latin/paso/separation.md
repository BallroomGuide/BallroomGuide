<a id="caping">Caping Walk Ending to the Separation</a>
-------------------------------------------------------

### Man

Having danced steps 1-8 of the Separation and led the Lady to veer to her left over steps 5-8, commence in Closed Position with L to R Hand Hold

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Steps** | **Turn** | **Alignment** | **Moving** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 9 | Hold position with feet together |  | Facing Wall |  |
| 10 | Hold position with feet together |  | Facing Wall |  |
| 11 | Hold position with feet together |  | Facing Wall |  |
| 12 | Hold position with feet together |  | Facing Wall |  |
| 13 | Hold position with feet together |  | Facing Wall |  |
| 14 | Hold position with feet together |  | Facing Wall |  |
| 15 | RF to side |  | Facing Wall | Against LOD |
| 16 | LF closes to RF |  | Facing Wall | Under Body |

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Timing** | **Position** | **Shape** | **Elevation** | **Footwork** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 9 | 1 |  |  | Medium | Feet Flat |
| 10 | 2 |  |  | Medium | Feet Flat |
| 11 | 3 |  |  | Medium | Feet Flat |
| 12 | 4 | LSP |  | Medium | Feet Flat |
| 13 | 5 |  |  | Medium | Feet Flat |
| 14 | 6 |  |  | Medium | Feet Flat |
| 15 | 7 | Closed |  |  | B |
| 16 | 8 | Closed |  |  | B |

1\) A high, medium, or low elevation may be used during steps 15-16  
 2) If a medium or low elevation is used, the footwork BF may be used on steps 15-17  
 3) A PP or CPP shape may be used during steps 15-16

### Lady

Having danced steps 1-8 of the Separation and veered leftward over steps 5-8, commence in Closed Position with L to R Hand Hold

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Steps** | **Turn** | **Alignment** | **Moving** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 9 | LF fwd, circling around the Man | 1/8 to R | Facing DC | DC |
| 10 | RF fwd, circling around the Man | 1/8 to R | Facing LOD | LOD |
| 11 | LF fwd, circling around the Man | 1/8 to R | Facing DW | DW |
| 12 | RF fwd, circling around the Man | 1/8 to R | Facing Wall | Wall |
| 13 | LF fwd, circling around the Man | 1/8 to R | Facing DW against LOD | DW against LOD |
| 14 | RF fwd, circling around the Man | 1/8 to R | Facing against LOD | Against LOD |
| 15 | LF to side | 1/4 to R between 14-15 | Backing Wall | Against LOD |
| 16 | RF closes to LF |  | Backing Wall | Under Body |

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Timing** | **Position** | **Shape** | **Elevation** | **Footwork** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 9 | 1 |  |  |  | B |
| 10 | 2 |  |  |  | B |
| 11 | 3 |  |  |  | B |
| 12 | 4 | LSP |  |  | B |
| 13 | 5 |  |  |  | B |
| 14 | 6 |  |  |  | B |
| 15 | 7 | Closed |  |  | B |
| 16 | 8 | Closed |  |  | B |

1\) A high or medium elevation may be used during steps 9-14, and a high, medium, or low elevation may be used during steps 15-16  
 2) If a medium or low elevation is used, the footwork BF may be used on each step  
 3) A PP or CPP shape may be used during steps 15-16

---------------------------------------------------------------

### Man

Having danced steps 1-7 of the Separation, commence in almost Closed Position

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Steps** | **Turn** | **Alignment** | **Moving** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 8 | LF to side &amp; slightly back | 1/8 to R | Backing DC | LOD |
| 9 | RF crosses behind LF | 1/8 to R | Backing LOD | Under Body |
| 10 | LF to side &amp; slightly fwd | 1/4 to R | Facing Centre | Against LOD |
| 11 | RF to side &amp; slightly fwd | 1/4 to R | Facing LOD | Wall |
| 12 | LF back in CBMP | 1/8 to L | Backing DW against LOD | Wall |
| 13 | RF to side | 1/8 to R between 12-13 | Facing LOD | Wall |
| 14 | LF closes to RF |  | Facing LOD | Under Body |
| 15 | RF to side |  | Facing LOD | Wall |
| 16 | LF closes to RF |  | Facing LOD | Under Body |

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Timing** | **Position** | **Shape** | **Elevation** | **Footwork** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 8 | 8 | Closed | CPP | Medium | BF |
| 9 | 1 | Closed | CPP | Medium | BHB |
| 10 | 2 | Closed | CPP | Medium | BF |
| 11 | 3 | Closed | CPP | Medium | BF |
| 12 | 4 | Fallaway | PP | Medium | BF |
| 13 | 5 | Closed |  |  | B |
| 14 | 6 | Closed |  |  | B |
| 15 | 7 | Closed |  |  | B |
| 16 | 8 | Closed |  |  | B |

1\) A high, medium, or low elevation may be used during steps 13-16  
 2) If a medium or low elevation is used, the footwork BF may be used steps 13-16  
 3) A PP or CPP shape may be used during steps 13-16

### Lady

Having danced steps 1-7 of the Separation, commence almost in Closed Position

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Steps** | **Turn** | **Alignment** | **Moving** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 8 | RF fwd &amp; across | 1/8 to R | Facing DC | Under Body |
| 9 | LF to side &amp; slightly back | 1/8 to R | Facing LOD | Centre |
| 10 | RF fwd &amp; across | 1/4 to R | Facing Wall | Under Body |
| 11 | LF to side &amp; slightly back | 1/4 to R | Backing LOD | Wall |
| 12 | RF back in CBMP | 1/8 to R | Backing DW | Wall |
| 13 | LF closes to RF | 1/8 to L between 12-13 | Backing LOD | Wall |
| 14 | RF to side |  | Backing LOD | Under Body |
| 15 | LF closes to RF |  | Backing LOD | Wall |
| 16 | RF to side |  | Backing LOD | Under Body |

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Timing** | **Position** | **Shape** | **Elevation** | **Footwork** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 8 | 8 | Closed | CPP | Medium | BF |
| 9 | 1 | Closed | CPP | Medium | BF |
| 10 | 2 | Closed | CPP | Medium | BF |
| 11 | 3 | Closed | CPP | Medium | BF |
| 12 | 4 | Fallaway | PP | Medium | BF |
| 13 | 5 | Closed |  |  | B |
| 14 | 6 | Closed |  |  | B |
| 15 | 7 | Closed |  |  | B |
| 16 | 8 | Closed |  |  | B |
