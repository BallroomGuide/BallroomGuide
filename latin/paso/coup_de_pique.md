 </header><a id="1">Coup de Pique Method 1</a>
------------------------------------

**This method is used when commencing with the RF (Lady LF) to end with weight on the LF (Lady RF)**

### Man

Commence in Closed Position with Closed Hold, facing Wall

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Steps** | **Turn** | **Alignment** | **Moving** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | RF points fwd &amp; across without weight | 1/8 to L | Facing DW |  |
| 2 | RF closes to LF | 1/8 to R | Facing Wall | Under Body |
| 3 | LF back | 1/8 to L | Backing DC against LOD | Against LOD |
| 4 | RF closes to LF | 1/8 to R | Facing Wall | Under Body |
| 5 | Replace weight to LF |  | Facing Wall | Under Body |

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Timing** | **Position** | **Shape** | **Elevation** | **Footwork** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1 | Promenade | PP | Low | o/e of T |
| 2 | 2 | Closed | CPP | High | T |
| 3 | 3 | Fallaway | PP | Low | BF |
| 4 | &amp; | Closed |  | Med | B |
| 5 | 4 | Closed |  | Med | B |

1\) Steps 4-5 may be danced with a CPP shape and High elevation  
 2) Step 5 may be danced BF (unless using High elevation)  
 3) If commenced in Promenade Position, omit turn on step 1

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Steps** | **Turn** | **Alignment** | **Moving** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | RF closes to LF |  | Facing Centre | Under Body |
| 2 | LF points fwd &amp; across without weight | 1/8 to R | Facing DC |  |
| 3 | LF closes to RF | 1/8 to L | Facing Centre | Under Body |
| 4 | RF back | 1/8 to R | Backing DW against LOD | Against LOD |
| 5 | LF closes to RF | 1/8 to L | Facing Centre | Under Body |
| 6 | Replace weight to RF |  | Facing Centre | Under Body |

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Timing** | **Position** | **Shape** | **Elevation** | **Footwork** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | &amp; | Closed |  |  | B |
| 2 | 1 | Promenade | PP | Low | o/e of T |
| 3 | 2 | Closed | CPP | High | T |
| 4 | 3 | Fallaway | PP | Low | BF |
| 5 | &amp; | Closed |  | Med | B |
| 6 | 4 | Closed |  | Med | B |

1\) Step 5-6 may be danced with a CPP shape and High elevation  
 2) Step 6 may be danced BF (unless using High elevation)
