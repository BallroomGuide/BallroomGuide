 This figure uses Bounce Action

| 3 | 2 | S |  | BF |
| 4 | a | a |  | i/e of T |

| 6 | a | a |  | i/e of T |
| 7 | 2 | S | Closed | BF |

1\) The Man's steps are a [Circular Volta](shadow_circ_voltas.md)

 4) If ended in Open CPP, the Man and Lady will both turn 1/8 more throughout
