### Man

Commence in Shadow Position Facing Wall  
 This figure uses Bounce Action

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Steps** | **Turn** | **Alignment** | **Moving** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross) |  | Facing Centre | Under Body |
| 2 | RF to side &amp; slightly back, part weight, toe turned out |  | Pointing LOD | LOD |
| 3 | LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross) |  | Facing Centre | Under Body |
| 4 | RF to side &amp; slightly back, part weight, toe turned out |  | Pointing LOD | LOD |
| 5 | LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross) |  | Facing Centre | Under Body |
| 6 | RF to side &amp; slightly back, part weight, toe turned out |  | Pointing LOD | LOD |
| 7 | LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross) |  | Facing Centre | Under Body |

 | **Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#** | **Timing** | **Rhythm** | **Position** | **Footwork** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1 | S | Shadow | BF |
| 2 | a | a | Shadow | i/e of T |
| 3 | 2 | S | Shadow | BF |
| 4 | a | a | Shadow | i/e of T |
| 5 | 1 | S | Shadow | BF |
| 6 | a | a | Shadow | i/e of T |
| 7 | 2 | S | Shadow | BF |

1\) Throughout this figure, the LF points toward Centre, the RF points down LOD, and the body faces DC  
 2) This figure may be extended, repeating the Volta action (steps 2-3) for an extra two or four beats  
 3) The final two steps may be replaced by steps 2-3 of a [LF Bota Fogo](travel_bf_fwd.md)  
 4) (Gold) The first step and/or the third step may be held for an additional beat, with a strong upward stretch before the step and a dropping of energy on the step itself, known as the Dropped Volta
