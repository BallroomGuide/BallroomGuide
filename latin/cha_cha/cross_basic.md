<header>Cross Basic (Silver)
--------------------

 </header>Commence in Closed Position

### Man

1\) The distribution of turn is left to the interpretation of the dancers  
 2) The Man may incline his body to the left over steps 1-2, and to the right over steps 6-7  
 3) (Gold) Raise the left hand on step 5 to lead the Lady to dance a Spiral Action underarm, regaining hold on step 8

### Lady

 <table class="style1"> <tbody><tr> <td style="width:10%">****Step<span style="color:white">\_</span>\#****</td> <td style="width:10%">**Count**</td> <td style="width:30%">**Steps**</td> <td style="width:20%">**Position**</td> <td style="width:30%">**Turn**</td> </tr> <tr> <td>1</td> <td>2</td> <td>RF behind LF (Latin Cross)</td> <td>Closed</td> <td rowspan="3">1/4 to L</td> </tr> <tr> <td>2</td> <td>3</td> <td>Transfer weight to LF</td> <td>Closed</td> </tr> <tr> <td>3-5</td> <td>4&amp;1</td> <td> Turning [Side Chasse](../technique/c_side_chasse.html), RLR </td> <td>Closed</td> </tr> <tr> <td>6</td> <td>2</td> <td>LF in front of RF (Latin Cross)</td> <td>Closed</td> <td rowspan="3">1/4 to L</td> </tr> <tr> <td>7</td> <td>3</td> <td>RF back &amp; slightly side</td> <td>Closed</td> </tr> <tr> <td>8-10</td> <td>4&amp;1</td> <td> Turning [Side Chasse](../technique/c_side_chasse.html), LRL </td> <td>Closed</td> </tr> </tbody></table>

1\) The distribution of turn is left to the interpretation of the dancers  
 2) The Lady may incline her body to the right over steps 1-2, and to the left over steps 6-7  
 3) (Gold) Dance a Spiral Action under a raised R hand at the end of step 5, followed by a Forward Walk on step 6, and a Forward Walk Turning on step 7 to continue with the figure
